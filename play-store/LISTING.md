## Full description
*(max 4000 characters)*

```
PJ Loury — Drone Videography turns your TV into a living window onto some of the world's most beautiful places.

A handcrafted, ad-free ambient experience: 50+ aerial drone clips shot in 4K from California, Hawaii, Utah, Spain, Italy, France, Germany, Austria, Croatia, Patagonia, and more. Each clip cross-fades into the next at a slow, meditative pace, captioned with its location.

• Cinematic 1080p — every clip color-graded and encoded for smooth high-quality playback
• Curated rotation — Cities, Coastal, Mountains, and Desert sections, or shuffle the entire collection
• A live mini-map shows where each clip was filmed
• Designed for Google TV — full D-pad navigation, immersive fullscreen, no menus to fight with
• No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Open it and forget about it.

Perfect as a digital fireplace for cocktail parties, a gallery wall in a quiet living room, an ambient backdrop while you read, or just because the world looks better from above.

All footage shot personally by PJ Loury. The clips loop continuously — leave it running and you'll see Big Sur at dawn, snow on Tahoe treetops, the Golden Gate Bridge under fog, Mont Saint-Michel at high tide, the salt flats of Bonneville, the cathedral of Salzburg, the Patagonian Andes, and dozens more.

Built as a thin native wrapper around drones.pjloury.com so updates ship to the TV automatically — new locations get added to the rotation as they're filmed.

Controls:
• Left / Right (D-pad): previous / next clip
• OK / Center: next clip
• Up / Down: toggle the title card
• Back: exit

Internet connection required — videos stream from the cloud, the app itself is small.
```
