DESCRIPTION  (max 4000 characters)

Aerial Landscapes turns any screen into a calm, beautiful window on the world.

Sit back and drift over glittering city skylines, crashing coastlines, snow-capped peaks, and sun-baked desert canyons — all captured from above in crisp, cinematic drone footage. It's the perfect ambient backdrop for your living room, your desk, or a quiet moment to yourself.

Now available on iPhone and iPad, and on Apple TV.

FEATURES

- A curated collection of breathtaking aerial drone videos from around the globe — San Francisco, Maui, Yosemite, the Dalmatian Coast, the Albanian Alps, Utah's red rock country, and many more.

- Four hand-picked categories: Cities, Coastal, Mountains, and Desert. Choose a mood, or shuffle them all.

- Smooth, gentle transitions between clips for a seamless, screensaver-like flow.

- Each location is labeled, so you always know where in the world you're looking.

- AirPlay support: send the footage straight to your Apple TV or AirPlay-enabled display with a single tap. The AirPlay button appears automatically when a compatible display is nearby.

- Clean, distraction-free design — just the footage, beautifully presented.

- Streams efficiently so you can enjoy fresh scenery without filling up your device.

Whether you want a tranquil escape, an elegant ambient display for guests, or simply a more beautiful screen, Aerial Landscapes brings the world to you — one stunning flight at a time.
